Kaden Foy’s Project Portfolio

I created this web page to showcase some of my personal engineering projects. I am passionate about mechanical design and have recently been incorporating mechatronics in my project work. Through my work as an engineer, I have gained experience in 3D Computer Aided Design (CAD) as well as other design techniques such as Finite Element Analysis (FEA).

CAL 3D Printer

I am developing a new technology that can print 3D parts significantly faster than traditional 3D printers.

Balancing Robot

I am creating a two-legged robot that can balance on its own set of wheels.

Acoustic Resonator

An acoustic resonator that shows the gradient of a vibrating membrane via a laser.

Hydrofoiling Sailboat

AInspired by the America Cup’s AC75 sailboats, I modelled a one-meter model that will eventually hydrofoil via wind power alone.

Couch Clip

After purchasing a 3D printer my first project was to create an overengineered couch clip that can withstand approximately 150lbs pounds of force.

Dark Matter Research

I have spent the last year of my career working on highly sensitive particle detectors that search for dark matter.
