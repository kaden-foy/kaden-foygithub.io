Hydroifoiling Sailboat

The America Cup for sailing has evolved substantially over the years. The boats have not only gotten a lot faster but also have an increasing amount of tech built into them now. After seeing the new AC75 boat designs for the Americas cup I have started to design my own mini AC75 RC boat that will hopefully hydrofoil using only wind (no props). I have 3D printed the haul and have started to play around with gearing mechanisms. The idea behind the mechanism is to control both front hydrofoils with only one servo motor.

While discussion the project with a friend he told me about Geneva gears. And I now am using them as the solution for the allowing one motor to control two foils.
